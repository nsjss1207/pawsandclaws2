Our journey so far

“Alone we can do so little, together we can do so much" is a something we have taken up to our hearts. Paws and Claws, our student led organization which has not only helped numerous strays and thousands of animals but also uplifted multiple human beings in its three years of working which we compeleted on the 18th June 2022. The support and effort that we have recieved from our team of volunteers has helped us achieve alot in these past three years.

From making beds and clothes for our furry friends during winter to our "Project Suraksha" where we had vaccinated many people to prevent the danger covid 19 carries with itself, from Christmas drives, where our volunteers packed gifts for young children and distributed the same to Educational drives, from blanket distribution drives to face masks and shield distribution drives, from helping in adopting of animals to drives of humanities where we joined hands with all student led organizations and bought them to work together for one single aim, we have done it all and we are glad that we have accomplished this much as an organization. With each year passing by, our team of volunteers is growing stronger. We hope to do more this year, and every year coming towards us. We have never been more thankful for the support and love we have recieved and we will always follow our will to do better as an organization.
